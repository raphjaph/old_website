## Ingredients 

- Canned Tuna (in it's own juice)
- Tomatoes (chopped)
- Canned Maize (sweet corn)
- Olive Oil
- Lemon Juice
- Salt & Pepper
- Chopped onions
- chopped garlic

## Steps

1. Put it all in a bowl, mix and enjoy!
